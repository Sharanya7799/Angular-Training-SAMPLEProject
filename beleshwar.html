Beleswar Beach

Situated on the Puri-Konark marine drive, about 15 kms towards Konark in Ouri district of Odisha, Beleswar town is popular for its beautiful beach and Shiva temple. The long and sandy Beleswar beach is perfect for sunbathing, swimming as well as enjoying the sunrise and sunset. The beach is enclosed by trees that make it ideal for picnic and photoshoots. The beach is also visited by the newly wedded couples seeking blessings at the nearby Shiva temple.
