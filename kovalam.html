Kovalam Beach

Kovalam is a village located 17 km from Trivandrum City in Kerala. The world famous Kovalam Beach is situated in this area of Trivandrum City. It consists of three beaches namely the Lighthouse Beach, Hawa Beach and Samudra Beach. All these beaches are extremely beautiful which explains why they attract several tourists all year round.
