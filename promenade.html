Promenade Beach

The paradise-like Promenade Beach in Puducherry is best for nature-walkers, photographers and beach lovers. It stretches 1.5 km along the rock-lined walkway in town and covers a lot of historical and cultural landmarks like the Statue of Joan of Arc, the Old Heritage Hall, the old lighthouse etc. It has ample eateries and restaurants where you can enjoy a delicious bite and sip refreshing drinks whilst enjoying beholding the spectacular sea.
